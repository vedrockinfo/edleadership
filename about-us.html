Join Us in Pioneering Change

The crises in education is the crises of pedagogy. It is already past half-time for SDG4. Yet, a quality education is implausible without first addressing the problem of foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN).

We need an evidencesupported transformative pedagogy that is capable of addressing FLN at both speed and scale; a pedagogy that is both holistic and inclusive, and that addresses climate change, and that can be easily integrated in existing systems.

A Global Learning Lab

The launch of the Global Learning Lab is an opportunity to not only witness the new pedagogy in action, but to go back empowered to scale this up in your own contexts.

Universal Literacy

A systemwide implementation of this transformative pedagogy is the surest path to universal literacy and to ensuring that all children receive a quality education within the systems.

We invite your active participation and catalytic role.
